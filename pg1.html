Welcome To Bankker

Bank apps can be beneficial for a number of reasons, including:
Convenience: You can access your account and perform banking tasks 24/7, without having to visit a branch.
Security: Banking apps use encryption and other security features to protect your information. You can also set up multi-factor authentication, such as a PIN or fingerprint, to further secure your account.
Real-time notifications: You can receive alerts about account activity, payment reminders, and suspicious transactions.
Reduced paper waste: You can opt for digital statements and reduce your carbon footprint.
Investment options: You can invest in fixed deposits, recurring deposits, stocks, and mutual funds.
Account management: You can update your profile information, lock or unlock your credit cards, and more.
Bill payments: You can pay bills, automate bill payments, and more.
Account history: You can review your banking statements and recent transaction history.
Customer care: You can reach out to customer care helplines through the app.
